# Dry-Powder
A rule-based dry powder calculator for Indian equity investors. Deploy capital as Nifty falls — without timing the market.


Most retail investors do exactly the wrong thing during corrections — they panic at −10%, freeze at −20%, and capitulate at −30%. The dry powder calculator solves this by forcing you to pre-commit your deployment plan when markets are calm.
You decide once: at each level of Nifty drawdown from its all-time high, you deploy a fixed percentage of your liquid capital. When the market actually falls, you don't think — you execute. The framework includes a permanent 10% reserve for tail-risk scenarios, an allocation visualizer to check whether you're sitting on too much cash, and Nifty-target levels so you know exactly when each tranche fires.
Built for Indian equity investors. Not financial advice — just a tool that turns market fear into a checklist.
